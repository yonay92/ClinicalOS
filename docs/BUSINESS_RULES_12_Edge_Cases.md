# BUSINESS RULES 12 - Edge Cases

## Study Closed
- No new subjects.
- Existing data remains available.
- Regulatory archived.

## Subject Early Termination
- Cancel future visits.
- Close pending charts as appropriate.
- Preserve history.

## Lost To Follow Up
- Stop future scheduling.
- Record timeline.
- Notify study team.

## Protocol Amendment
- Never overwrite previous templates.
- Generate comparison.
- Require approval.

## System Failure
- Retry queued automations.
- Log failures.
- Notify administrators if execution cannot complete.
